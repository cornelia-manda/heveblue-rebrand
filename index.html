HEVEBLUE

Sole Lead Designer / Brand Strategist / Creative Director / Social Media Strategist

A strategic redesign of the K-Beauty brand HEVEBLUE, repositioning clinical products into an aspirational "Blinding Glow" superpower.

❮ ❯

The Big Idea

The rebrand shifts the narrative from clinical problem-solving to unlocking confidence. Inspired by founder Oh Min-cho’s personal struggle with sensitive skin, HEVEBLUE is repositioned as a "Secret Weapon" for transformation.

❮ ❯

Visual Identity

I designed a fluid, cursive "h" with a radiant sunburst to visualize the promise of breathtaking radiance.

  • Typography: Contralto Big & Stroudley
  • Palette: Dreamy Pastels & Radiant Light
❮ ❯

Creative Process

Adhering to a GenAI-first methodology, over 50% of the project was created using AI tools. Using Adobe Firefly Pro and Photoshop, I generated custom imagery for a unique identity.

❮ ❯

Social Strategy

The strategy centers on radical inclusivity. By launching the #BlindingGlowChallenge, we use real customer progress to showcase authentic transformation stories.
